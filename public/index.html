Candidate Statement


There are approximately 12000 reasons I am seeking re-election to the Vacaville Unified School District Board - the students. My voting record demonstrates I am passionate and committed to the education of all students. I have been in the Vacaville District forty years, as a teacher, administrator, after school director, board member and parent. This long history allows me to share my unique perspective and expertise with other Board members and informs all of my decisions.

Being fiscally responsible, I have voted to use resources to recruit and retain excellent staff, keep facilities safe, clean and updated, and purchase needed technology. I have voted to expand our vocational pathways, provide additional AP classes, address bullying and increase mental health services. I support programs of choice including our traditional K-12 schools, a new K-8 school, a new K-12 Independent Study Charter, both an elementary and high school charter and two alternative programs, Alternative Cooperative Education (ACE) and Spanish Immersion (SPICE).

If re-elected I will ensure all Vacaville Unified School District students achieve success in a district that has safe schools, a positive culture, excellent instruction, programs of choice including vocational and academic pathways, and fiscal security.
